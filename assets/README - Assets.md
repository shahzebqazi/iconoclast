# Website assets — deliverables

## Brand

- Logo (primary, horizontal, stacked)
- Logo reversed / on dark backgrounds
- Logo monochrome / single-color
- Color palette (primary, secondary, neutrals, semantic)
- Typography spec (families, weights, scale)

## Icons & favicons

- Favicon (ICO and/or SVG)
- Apple touch icon
- PWA / manifest icons (if applicable)
- UI icon set or sprite (SVG)

## Imagery

- Hero and key section images (formats and sizes agreed)
- Product / feature screenshots
- Team or brand photography (if used)
- Illustrations or decorative graphics
- Image alt text / captions list (content deliverable)

## Video & audio (if applicable)

- Hosted or embeddable video files
- Poster frames / thumbnails
- Transcripts and captions

## SEO & social

- Open Graph image(s)
- Twitter / X card image(s)
- Default share title and description copy

## Content (non-visual but asset-adjacent)

- Final copy for all main pages
- CTA labels and microcopy
- Legal pages: privacy, terms, cookie policy (as required)

## Email & offline (optional)

- Branded email header or template graphics
- Linked PDFs or downloadable one-pagers

## Handoff to development

- Source files (e.g. Figma links or exports)
- Named exports with agreed naming convention
- List of required breakpoints or max widths per image slot
