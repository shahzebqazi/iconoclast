Ritual

Voice, intent, and why this work exists.

Who this is

Iconoclast Audio is an extreme-music production practice: amps that buzz, drums that feel like weather, vocals that survived the session, and low end that refuses to disappear. If your art is polite, we can still make it loud—but the best work starts when there is something to fight for.

“Ritual” is the word because mastering here is not a conveyor belt. It is repeated passes, level-matched honesty, and a refusal to ship a master that only works on one pair of speakers. You bring the performance; we finish the pressure wave.

What we do

Production, session bass, session guitar, mixing, stereo mastering, stem work when the mix demands surgical moves, and blunt feedback when the song is fighting itself. Extreme Metal Mastering is the center of gravity.

This is not a template house. Chains move per project. The goal is always the same: your record should feel finished, not laminated.

Aura

You will find opinionated choices, visible enthusiasm for ugly tones done right, and zero interest in fake “analog warmth” copy. If you want safe and beige, there are plenty of options. If you want something that still hurts in the right way after the limiter—stay.
