How booking works

  1. Email first. Send what you have (rough length, format, deadline fantasy vs reality). We reply when we can—no ticket system, just humans.
  2. Files. Prefer 24-bit WAV or AIFF; include reference level notes if you care. Stems only when agreed—mastering is not a remix service by default.
  3. Revisions. One round of substantive tweaks is normal if we missed intent; endless ping-pong is not. Be specific; we respond in kind.
